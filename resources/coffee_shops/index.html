I pick my coffee shops according to: wifi, coffee, music and neighborhood. I love coffee shops, because for me it's the best way to find isolation while still being "social"

Name Stars Neighborhood Wifi Speed Thoughts
Devocion 5 Williamsburg 5 Sun roof; plants everywhere; Ragae and Classic Rock music; large open space. My top favorite.
Starbucks 4 Williamsburg 5 I rarely advocate anyone to go to Starbucks. This one is an exception. The wifi is powered by Google, they have an espresso machine and a huge space and great seating. I only get tea there. Coffee is great.
Toby's Estate 4 Williamsburg 3.5 Gets great sunlight. I mostly like it because of their coffee. Plus it is usually the only coffee shop in Willi that has wifi on weekends.
Madeline 5 Flatbush 5 It's a short bike ride away from me, has great breakfast options. Tasty espressos. If you don't find a spot immediately, wait 5 min.
Milk & Honey Cafe 4 Flatbush 4 An open space with good light, many tables and tasty coffee. Music is meh, bring headphones. Great food.
