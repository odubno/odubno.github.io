Meetups


I pick my coffee shops according to: wifi, coffee, music and neighborhood. I love coffee shops, because for me it's possibly the best way to get work done

I use workhardanywhere to find my coffee shops and CUPS to pay for my coffee


Name Stars Neighborhood Wifi Speed Thoughts
Devocion 5 Williamsburg 5 Sun roof; plants everywhere; Raggae and Classic Rock music; large open space. My top favorite.
Starbucks 4 Williamsburg 5 I rarely advocate anyone to go to Starbucks. This one is an exception. The wifi is powered by Google, they have an espresso machine and a huge space and great seating. I only get tea there. Coffee is great.
Toby's Estate 4 Williamsburg 3.5 Gets great sunlight. I mostly like it because of their coffee. Plus it is usually the only coffee shop in Willi that has wifi on weekends.
Madeline 5 Flatbush 5 It's a short bike ride away from me, has great breakfast options. Tasty espressos. If you don't find a spot immediately, wait 5 min.
Milk & Honey Cafe 4 Flatbush 4 An open space with good light, many tables and tasty coffee. Music is meh, bring headphones. Great food.
Hungry Ghost 5 Prospect Heights 4.5 They serve Stumptown. Great coffee! Classic rock and jazz. Awesome baristas. No outlets. reserved sections for laptops. Standing tables for work.
Bean and Bean 4.5 Chelsea 3.5 Large open space. Tasty coffee. Music leans more towards pop. Many students from FIT.
Pushcart 4.5 Chelsea 4 Homey feel. Neighborhood driven. An ok space. Provides enough for me to spend several hours working there.
